# Brandon Timinsky

CEO & Founder of ZAR. Based in Amsterdam.

Building stablecoin infrastructure so a billion people in countries with unstable money can hold, spend, and move digital dollars.

Previously founded SadaPay (acquired 2024) and GasNinjas (acquired 2017).

University of Miami alumnus.

zar

# ZAR

Connecting cash economies to digital dollars across Asia, Africa, and Latin America.

## What We're Building

A self-custodial wallet that gives people access to digital dollars, a Visa debit card, and a merchant network for local cash conversion. No bank account required.

## Funding

$20M raised to date.

  • Series A ($12.9M): a16z crypto, Dragonfly, VanEck, Coinbase Ventures, Endeavor Catalyst
  • Seed: Solana founders, additional angels

## Markets

Pakistan, Bangladesh, Ethiopia, DR Congo, Tanzania, Madagascar, Kenya, Uganda, Angola, Lebanon, Argentina, and expanding.

## Launch

October 2025. Currently iterating toward product-market fit.

# SadaPay

A digital payments company in Pakistan. Founded it, spent 5 years building it from zero to acquisition.

## The Challenge

Pakistan has one of the most complex regulatory environments in fintech. Capital controls, banking restrictions, and a largely cash-based economy.

## The Outcome

Built a neobank that scaled operations in-country. Navigated regulatory complexity across multiple stakeholders.

Acquired in 2024.

# GasNinjas

Acquired in 2017.
